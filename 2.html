Dental Implaments

  • What are Dental Implants in India?

    • A dental implant provides strength to a tooth. It is literally an artificial tooth root that is placed into the jawbone to hold a replacement like a single crown (single tooth) or bridge (multiple teeth) or even a denture. Like tooth roots, dental implants are secured in the jawbone and are not visible once surgically placed. They are made of titanium, which is lightweight, strong and biocompatible so that they are not rejected by the body. The next best thing to healthy, natural teeth.
